Arms-Impaled

Arms-Impaled. A term to express the arms of a man and his wife, called Baron and Femme. The Shield is divided by a perpendicular line. The Husbands arms are placed on the dexter side and the wiles on the sinister as P. 10, f. 21, and P. 46, f. 1.

The arms of office are impaled in the sameway. See Arms of Office.
