Balance

Balance, An apparatus for weighing bodies; a beam with two opposite scales. P. 39, f. 22.

This is usually, though incorrectly, blazoned apair of Scales, whereas the scales are the two bowls attached to the end of the beam which together with them makes up the Balance and are said to be equally poised.
