Baron and Femme

Baron and Femme. Husband and wife.

The Arms are borne impaled, the husband'son the dexter and the wife's on the sinister, as P. 10, f. 21. If the woman is an Heiress, or Co-Heiress, her Arms are borne in an Escutcheon of Pretence; as P. 11, f. 21.
