Books

Books. Are variously borne in Coat Armour, e.g. A Book expanded, or open, a Book closed garnished and clasped. P. 43, f. 31.

On a Book open and garnished, on thedexter side seven seales, the words " Sapientia felicitas," sometimes " Dominus illuminatio mea," as in the Arms of the University of Oxford, f. 32.
