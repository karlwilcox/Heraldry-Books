Caboshed

Caboshed, Caboched, Cabossed, or Cabosed. Terms to express the heads of Deer, Bulls, Goats, etc., when cut off and set full faced, without any part of the neck being left. P. 28, f. 34, 38, and 58.

Cabossed does not apply to a Leopard's face.P. 28, f. 4.
