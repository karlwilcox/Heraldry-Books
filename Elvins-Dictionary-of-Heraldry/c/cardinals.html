Cardinal's Cap

Cardinal's Cap, or Hat. P. 40, f. 60.

A Cardinal's Hat is red, The Archbishops of France bear a hat of this description overtheir arms, but its colour is vert, and it has only four rows of tassels ; Abbots bear the same sable with three rows of tassels.
