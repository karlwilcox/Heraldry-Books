Clarion

Clarion, Sufflue, Claricord, or Rest. P. 43, f. 27 and 28.

This is by some supposed to denote the restfor the lance, but this cannot be the case as Clarions are found in armoury b if ore the rest for the lance was invented. In fact the very name sufflue, and clarion, point to some kind of wind instrument.
