Coil

Coil. A Coil of Flax, P. 43, f. 13. as borne in the Crest of Washbourne. Coins. The Heraldry that may be learned from British and Foreign Coins is of the utmost value, since it is always historically correct. Coiled. Turned round, or twisted ; as an Adder coiled. P. 30, f. 42.
