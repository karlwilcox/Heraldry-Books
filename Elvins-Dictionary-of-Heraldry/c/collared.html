Collared

Collared. A term applied to animals, wmen they have a plain collar round the neck ; if a line or chain is attached to the collar, it is termed collared and lined, or chained. P. 18, f. 21.

If any animal has any kind of Coronet roundits neck, it is tei-med ' Gorged," as a Demi lion ramp, gorged with a ducal coronet. r. 26, f. 31 ; P. 19, f. 21.
