Cottised flory

Cottised flory, or a Fesse between two barrulets flory. Flory means only the top of the fleur-de-lis. Flory counter flory is the top and bottom of the fleur-de-lis placed alternately as P. 5, f. 14.

See Fesse Flory and Fess Flory counterflory. P. 3, f. 19 and 20.
