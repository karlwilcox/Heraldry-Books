Counter Flory

Counter Flory, Counter-Fleury, Contre Fleure, or Contrefleuronne. When the edges of anything are charged with fleur-de-lis, alternately placed, as the tressure flory counterflory. P. 35, f. 16. See P. 3, f. 20. P. 17, f. 14. A Fesse and Bend Flory Counter-flory.
