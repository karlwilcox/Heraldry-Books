Cross Fusily

Cross Fusily. Properly seven fusils conjoined in cross extending to the edges of the shield. P. 8, f. 3. A cross fusily is a cross covered with fusils of alternate colours in the same way as a cross lozengy. P. 8, f. 2. Although the former is by many called a cross fusily but incorrectly.
