Crown Civic

Crown Civic, or Wreath. A garland composed of oak-leaves and acorns. P. 24, f. 39.

The Corona Civica was among the Romans,the highest military reward, assigned to him who had preserved the life of a citizen. It bore the inscription " Ob civem servatum."
