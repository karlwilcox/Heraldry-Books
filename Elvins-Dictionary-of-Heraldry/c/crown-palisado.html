Crown Palisado

Crown Palisado, or Vallary. Also termed Vallairie, Corona castrensis ; is depicted differently as shewn at P. 24, f. 17 and 37.

It is given to those who first enter theentrenchment of an enemy. The term Vallary is derived from the Latin Vallum.

The Crowns Ducal, Eastern, Mural, Naval,and Palisado, may ho of any tincture and placed on the Helmet with or without a wreath.

They are also very frequently placed on theheads, or round the necks of Crests and iSupporters.
