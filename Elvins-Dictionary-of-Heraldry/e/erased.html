Erased

Erased, or Erazed. Torn off, having a jagged edge as a Lion's head erased. P. 26, f. 25 and 38.

It also expresses the lower part of the neckwhen of a different colour from the rest. P. 28, f. 3. Observe if the head were perfesse the partition line would be in the centre.
