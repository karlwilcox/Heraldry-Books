Falcon

Falcon, or Hawk In Heraldry is always represented close as P. 33, f 31, if not mentioned to the contrary, and usually borne with bells on its legs, when it is termed a Falcon belled

If represented with hood, virols, or rings,and leashes, then it is said to be hooded, jessed, and leashed, and the colours must be named. For examples of Falcons see P. 33, f. 31 to 35.
