Flanched

Flanched. Glover gives as the arms of a natural son of one of the Fitzalans, Ralph de Arundel, a shield of Fitz-Alan, flanched ar. ; that is, a shield ar., having flanches of Fitzalan and Warrenne quarterly. P. 2, f. 48.
