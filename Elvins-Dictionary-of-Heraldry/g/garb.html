Garb

Garb. A sheaf of Wheat. P. 45, f. 14.

If the sheaf is of any other grain, the particular grain must be named, and when thestraw is of a different tincture to the ears, it must be mentioned ; as a Garb or. Eared ppr. A Garb fesseways. P. 14, f. 21. Gu. three Garbs ar. a bordure sa. bezantee. The arms of Clement of Lincolnshire and Norfolk.
