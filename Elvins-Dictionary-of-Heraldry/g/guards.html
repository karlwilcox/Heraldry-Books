Guards

Guards. Rows of Fur upon the dexter side of Peer's Iniantles and denote the rank, viz. Four guards for a Duke, Three and half for a Marquis. See P. 35, f. 16. Three for an Earl. Two and a half for a Viscount. And Two for a Baron.
