Gyronne

Gyronne, Gyronny or Gyrony.The field is said to be Gyronny when divided into several Gyrons as gyronne of six, of eight, of twelve, of sixteen. P. 2, f. 25, 26, 29 and 30. Gyronny of eight within a bordure. P. 19, f. 41.
