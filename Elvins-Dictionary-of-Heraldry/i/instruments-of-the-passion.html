Instruments of the Passion

Instruments of the Passion. Displayed on shields in ecclesiastical decorations are the Pitcher, the Towel, Sword, Scourge, Crown of Thorns, Reed and Sponge, Spear, Nails, Ladder, Pincers, and a Heart pierced with five wounds, arranged in different ways.
