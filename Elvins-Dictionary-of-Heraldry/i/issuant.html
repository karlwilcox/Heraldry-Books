Issuant

Issuant, or Issuing. Terms which signify proceeding from, or coming out of, as a Lion issuing out of a fesse. P. 26, f. 26, and f. 29. An Estoile issuant out of a Crescent. P. 23, f. 39. Rays issuing, f. 30.
