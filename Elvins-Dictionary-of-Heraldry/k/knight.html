Knight

Knight. A Title of Honour next to that of a Baronet, not hereditary. Knights may be divided into two classes. First ; those who are invested with some Order, and bear their Arms as P. 16, f. 21. Secondly; Those who are not so invested, termed Knight Bachelors. The arms of a Knight Bachelor is distinguished by the Helmet. P. 13, f. 21.
