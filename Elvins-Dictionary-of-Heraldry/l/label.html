Label

Label, Lambeaux, or File. A Mark of Cadency used to distinguish the arms of the eldest son. See Disiinition of Houses. P. 46.

All the Members of the Royal Family usethe Label extending across the shield, each being charged with different figures, except that of the Prince of Wales, which is plain. P. 19. f. 40 to 45.
