Laurel

Laurel. The leaves, sprigs and branches of which are of common. use in Coat Armour,

A Laurel-leaf. P. 45, f. 18, A Laurel Sprigand Branch. P. 44, f. 40. The Triumphal Crown is composed of laurel ; it is sometimes blazoned a Chapiet of laurel. P. 24, f. 34.
