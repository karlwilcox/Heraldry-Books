Leash

Leash. A band wherewith to bind anything ; also a leather thong with a button at the end, by which Falconers (having run it through the varvels) hold the Hawk fast upon the hand. P. 33, f. 34

The term is also applied to the line whichpasses from the collar of one greyhound to another.
