Limpago

Limpago. The engraving, P. 26, f. 55, is a copy of a Limpago given in Burke's Heraldic Illustrations.

Viz : a Lion's body with human face andflowing hair.

It is generally represented as a Lion's body,the face of a man with the scalp and horns of a Bull. P. 27. f. 54.
