Lobster

Lobster. A crustaceous animal, sometimes used in Coat Armour, and is borne by the name Banester, Dikes, etc. P. 32, f. 57. Lobsters-claws in Saltire, f. 56, borne by the name of Tregarthick, Kerne, etc.
