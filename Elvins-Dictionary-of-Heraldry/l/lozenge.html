Lozenge

Lozenge. The Lozenge is a rhomboidal figure that has equal sides, and unequal angles as P. 40, f. 14.

The arms of all Maidens and Widows areborne in a Lozenge. See P. 9, f. 21, P. 22, f. 21, and P. 46, f. 9, 10, 11, 24, and 32.
