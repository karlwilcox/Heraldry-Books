Marined

Marined. a term used for an animal with the lower parts of the body like a fish, as a Sea-lion. P. 26, f. 53. Most animals are found so joined to the tail of a fish, and are blazoned a Sea-horse. P. 29, f. 55. A Sea-unicorn, a Sea-Wolf, Sea-Bear, etc.
