Mi-Taille

Mi-Taille. A term to express that the escutcheon is cut only half way across, in bend sinister. If divided dexterwavs it is termed Mi-tranche.

These divisional lines, together withthose called Mi-party, and Mi-couppe, form three gyrons. P. 19, f. 43.
