Orle

Orle. One of the subordinaries is composed of lines passing round the shield, forming an inner border, and derive its name from Ourler to hem. P. 5, f. 31. The Orle is subject to all the accidental forms of Lines as Engrailed, Invecked, etc. f. 32.

Orle of clouds. P. 22, f. 24.

Orle of three pieces. P. 22, f. 25.

Orle fretted with a pallet. P. 5, f. 34.

In Orle. P. 5. f. 30.

Within an Orle. P. 5. f. 38.

An Orle of Estoiles. P. 5, f. 36.

Double Orle. f. 33.
