Papal-Crown

Papal-Crown, Tiara, or Triple-Crown. A long red cap, surmounted by a mound and cross pattee ; round this cap are three Marquesses coronets of gold, placed one above the other ; from the inside issue two ribbons fringed, P. 40, f. 59.
