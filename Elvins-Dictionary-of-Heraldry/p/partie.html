Partie

Partie, or Party. Signifies divided, applied to all divisions of the field, or any figure when divided by those particular lines, as Party per pale. Party per fesse, etc. P. 2, f. 1 to 10 ; P. 4, f. 32 to 39 ; P. 16, f. 30 to 32 ; and P. 19. f. i to 25.
