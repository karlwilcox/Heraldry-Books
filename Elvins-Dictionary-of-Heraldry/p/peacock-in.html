Peacock in his pride

Peacock in his pride. P. 34, f. 4 ; and Peacock close, f. 5.

The Peacock is used in ecclesiastical decoration, and symbolises power and omniscience.A Plume, or Panache of Peacock's feathers. P. 43, f. 42.
