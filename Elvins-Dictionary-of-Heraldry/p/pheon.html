Pheon

Pheon. The barbed head of a dart, or an arrow, frequently borne in Coats of Arms, depicted in various ways, but if not differently described is always represented as P. 37, f. 15.

A Pheon engrailed on outer edges, f. 19.

A Pheon mounted on a staff, and featheredalso, blazoned an arrow Pheoned. f. 14.

A Pheon per pale. P. 14, f. 40.
