Quintin

Quintin, or Quintal. An upright pole with a cross beam onthe top, which works on a pivot. At one end cf the cross beam is a shield painted with rings, and at the other end is a log of wood, suspended by a stout chain. Men on horseback tilted at the shield, and unless they passed very quickly were struck by the log as the beam revolved. P. 37, f. S9
