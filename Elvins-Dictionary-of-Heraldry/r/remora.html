Remora

Remora, or Fish Odimoliont. The Sucker-Fish.

In Fo. 103 Bossewell gives the Coat ofRoscarroche. Vert three scythes argent. For Crest, " the fishe odimoliont haryaut (haurient) sable.'

The dexter supporter to the arms of BaronScarsdale is a female figure, holding in the sinister hand a javelin, entwined with a Remora, which Burke and Foster in their Peerage's describe as a serpent.
