Seal

Seal. A device, or an engraved inscription ; also an impression made on wax.

Personal Seals may be regarded among themost trustworthy evidences of armorial bearings. Indeed, when a seal attached to a charter bears the same name as that of the person granting the charter, its authority for a shield of arms is almost indisputable.
