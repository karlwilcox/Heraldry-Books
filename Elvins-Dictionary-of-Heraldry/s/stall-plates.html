Stall-Plates

Stall-Plates. A square or oblong plate of gilt copper, upon which the Arms of Knights of the Garter and the Bath are emblazoned, and fixed in their stalls in the Chapels of St. George at Windsor, and of Henry Vh, at Westminster. The arms of the Esquires of the Knights are similarly displayed and recorded in the lower range of Stalls.
