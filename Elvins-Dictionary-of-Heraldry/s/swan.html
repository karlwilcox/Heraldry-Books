Swan

Swan. Always borne with the wings endorsed, unless it is expressed otherwise. A Swan close. P. 34, f. 27. A Demi Swan, wings expanded, f. 28. A Swan Rousant; i.e., standing with wings endorsed, f. 25. A Swan in Pride, is a Swan represented swimming, f. 25. A Swan's Head erased, f. 29.
