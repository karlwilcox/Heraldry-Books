Tower

Tower. Always depicted as P. 23, f. 8, unless differently blazoned.

A Tower is said to be Masoned when thecement is of a different tincture from the stones.

Az. a fess or. betw. three Towers ar. areborne by Dasent of Ascott. Berks.
