Victoria and Albert

Victoria and Albert, The Royal Order of. Badge. P. 24, f, 25.

This Order was Instituted 10th February,1882. Enlarged 10th October, 1834; 10th November, 1835 ; and 15th March, 1880.

The Imperial Order of the CrownOf India. Instituted 1st January, 1878, to commemorate the assumption of Her Majesty's Imperial title of Empress of India. Madge. P. 24, f. 30So.

These two Orders [including previous] are confined to Ladies,the Members are entitled to no special precedence.
