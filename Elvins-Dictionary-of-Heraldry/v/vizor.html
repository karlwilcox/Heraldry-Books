Vizor

Vizor, Garde-Visure, Beaver, or Beauvoir. That part of the helmet which defends the face, and which can be lifted up or put down at pleasure. A Helmet, with vizor raised. P. 38, f. 10. An Esquire's Helmet, with vizor down. P. 24, f. 12.
