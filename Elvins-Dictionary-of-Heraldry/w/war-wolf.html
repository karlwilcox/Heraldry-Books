War-Wolf

War-Wolf. Also termed a Were- Wolf, Wher-Wolf, or Wolf.Man, P. 30, f. 18. As borne by the name of Dickeson, or Dickison.

Supposed to be a man living a wolf's naturea genus I imagine far from being extinct.
