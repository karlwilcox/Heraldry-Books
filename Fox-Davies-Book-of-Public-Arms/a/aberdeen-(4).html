ABERDEEN, See of. Azure, in the porch of a church St Nicholas in pontificals, his right hand raised over three children in a cauldron surrounded by flames, in the left hand a pastoral staff, all proper (Woodward).

[This coat was never matriculated in Lyon Register.]
