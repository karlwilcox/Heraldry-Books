ADVENTURERS, New, or French Merchants. Barry wavy of six argent and azure, a chief quarterly gules and or, in the first and fourth, a lion passant guardant of the last, in the second and third two roses gules, seeded or, barbed vert ; over all on an inescutcheon azure, a sceptre in pale or. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, two anchors in saltire and a sceptre in pale all or. Srifporters — Two pegasi argent, with wings indorsed or, maned and hoofed of the last. Motto — " Reddite cuique suum."

[These arms were granted 13th November 1616 by Sir William Segar, Garter, and William Camden, Clarenceux.]
