ADVENTURERS, Merchant, or Hambrough Merchants. (This Society was incorporated 24 Edw. I., 1296, and obtained ample privileges, and a confirmation of their charter from Queen Elizabeth.) Barry nebulae of six argent and azure, a chief quarterly gules and or, in the first and fourth quarters a lion passant guardant of the fourth, in the second and third two roses gules barbed vert. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a pegasus current with wings indorsed argent. Supporters — Two pegasi with wings indorsed argent, each charged on the wing with three roses in pale gules. Motto — " Dieu nous adventure donne bonne."
