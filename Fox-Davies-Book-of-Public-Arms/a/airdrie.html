AIRDRIE (Lanarkshire). Has not matriculated any armorial bearings. Those used are for arms, argent, an eagle displayed with two heads sable, in chief a crescent . . . between two mullets pierced. Crest — A cock proper. Motto —

" Vigilantibus." These arms are taken from those of Aitchison.
