ALBERTA, Province of, Dominion of Canada. Azure, in front of a range of snow mountains proper, a range of hills vert, in base a wheatfield surmounted by a prairie both also proper, on a chief argent, a St George's Cross. [Assigned by Royal Warrant, 30th May 1907.]
