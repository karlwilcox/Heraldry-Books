ALNWICK (Northumberland). Has no armorial bearings. The seal of the County Council of Northumberland (118) displays as the arms of Alnwick, St Michael overcoming the dragon. The shield of St Michael is charged with a cross clechee instead of the ordinary cross similar to that of St George.
