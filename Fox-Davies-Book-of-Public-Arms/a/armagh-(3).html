ARMAGH, Archbishopric of. Azure, an episcopal staff ensigned with a cross patee or, surmounted by a pall argent, edged and fringed gold, charged with four crosses formee-fitchee sable.

[This coat, which is recorded in Ulster's Office, remains in use, but through the disestablishment of the Irish Church it is really extinct, and its use is illegal.]
