ARMSTRONG COLLEGE (Newcastle-upon-Tyne). Argent, a cross pat6e, quadrat in the centre gules, on a chief of the last three towers of the first, all within a bordure compony of the second and or, upon a canton the arms of Baron Armstrong. Crest — A tower, thereon a beacon fired all proper. Motto —

" Mens agitat molem."

[Granted, College of Arms, March 24, 1906. The arms of Lord Armstrong were "gules, a tilting spear fessewise or, headed argent between two dexter arms embowed in armour, couped at the shoulders fessewise proper, hands extended of the last."]
