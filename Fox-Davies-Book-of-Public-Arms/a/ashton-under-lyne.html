ASHTON-UNDER-LYNE (Lancashire). Has no armorial bearings. Those used are taken from the family of Ashton or Assheton, and are argent, a mullet pierced sable, in the dexter chief a crescent gules. Crest — On a mural coronet proper, a griffin's head erased gules, gorged with a ducal coronet or. Motto —

" Labor omnia vincit."
