ASTON MANOR, Borough of (Warwickshire). Quarterly azure and or, a cross moline between three crosses patee fitchee in the first and fourth quarters, and two lions passant in the second and third, all counterchanged. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, within an annulet or, a squirrel sejant cracking a nut proper. Motto — " E.xaltavit humiles."

[Granted, College of Arms, March 22, 1904.]
