BANCHORY (Co. Kincardine). Has no arms. The device upon the seal consists of three escutcheons : (a) argent, three holly leaves in chief vert, and in base a hunting horn sable stringed gules (Burnett of Leys) ; (b) Burnett of Leys as above impaling Ramsay of Balmain, viz., argent, an eagle displayed sable ; (c) azure on a fesse between three pheons argent, a stag lodged gules (Davidson of Inchmarlo).
