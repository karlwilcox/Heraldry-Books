BEWDLEY (Worcestershire). Argent, an anchor in pale azure, the anchor surmounted with a fetterlock or, on the dexter side of the anchor a sword erect of the second, hilt and pommel also or, on the sinister side of the anchor, a rose gules.

[Recorded in the College of Arms, at the Visitation of Worcester, 1634.]
