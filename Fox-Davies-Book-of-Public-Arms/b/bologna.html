BOLOGNA (Italy). Quarterly : i and 4, argent, a cross gules, on a chief azure, three fleurs-de-lis or separated by the points of a label of four points gules ; 2 and 3, azure, the word " Libertas," in letters of gold in bend (or bend sinister).
