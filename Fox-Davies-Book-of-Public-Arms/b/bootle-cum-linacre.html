BOOTLE-CUM-LINACRE (Lancashire). Argent, on a chevron between three fleurs-de-lis azure, as many stags' heads caboshed or, on a chief sable, three mural crowns of the field. Crest — Upon a rock a lighthouse proper. Motto — " Respice aspice prospice."

[Granted by Sir Albert William Woods, Knt, Garter Principal King of ArmS) Robert Laurie, Clarenceux King of Arms, Walter Aston Blount, Norroy King of Arms, November 4, 1869.]
