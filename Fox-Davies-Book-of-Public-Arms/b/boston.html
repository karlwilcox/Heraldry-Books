BOSTON (Lincolnshire). Sable, three coronets composed of crosses pattee and fleurs-de-lis in pale or. Crest — A woolpack charged with a ram couchant all proper. Supporters — On either side a mermaid proper, ducally crowned azure.

The arms, crest, and supporters were allowed and confirmed, ist December 1 568, by Robert Cook, Clarenceux King of Arms.
