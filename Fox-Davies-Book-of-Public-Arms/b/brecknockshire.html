BRECKNOCKSHIRE. Has no armorial bearings, but the following are used and appear on the seal of the County Council, namely, quarterly i and 4 sable, a fesse or, between two swords, that in chief point upwards, and that in base point downwards proper ; 2 and 3 or, three eagles displayed „„„„., [In an article on the County Council Seals in the County and Local and Government Magazine, by Allan VVyon, these are termed rere-mice (bats)]. Motto — " Undeb hedd llwyddiant " (Unity, peace, prosperity).
