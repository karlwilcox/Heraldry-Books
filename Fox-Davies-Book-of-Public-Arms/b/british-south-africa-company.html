BRITISH SOUTH AFRICA COMPANY. Gules, per fesse bezanty and semee of ears of wheat or, on a fesse wavy argent between two bulls statant in chief and an elephant in base all proper three lymphads with oars sable. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a lion passant guardant or, supporting with the dexter fore paw an elephant's tusk erect proper. Supporters — On either side a springbok proper.

[Recorded in College of Arms.]
