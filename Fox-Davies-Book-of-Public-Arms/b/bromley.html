BROMLEY, Borough of (Kent). Quarterly gules and azure, on a fesse wavy argent, three ravens volant proper, between in the first quarter two branches of broom slipped of the third, in the second a sun in splendour, in the third an escallop shell or, and in the fourth a horse forcene also argent. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, upon two bars wavy azure and a gent, an escallop shell as in the arms, between two branches of broom proper. Motto — " Dum cresco spero." [Granted, College of Arms, igth April 1904.]
