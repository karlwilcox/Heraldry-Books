BRUSSELS (Belgium). Gules, St Michael or, overthrowing the devil sable. Upon the escutcheon is placed a coronet of pearls and behind the shield which is supported by two lions or, standing on a natural compartment vert, two lances in saltire or, on each a flag fringed of the last, the dexter charged with the arms of Brabant (sable a lion rampant or) and the sinister with the same arms of Brussels.
