CALNE (or Cawne, Wiltshire). (Sable), a tower towered and domed (argent) between two feathers (of the last) each feather in an escrol (or), and a like feather in the gateway of the tower.

Recorded at the Visitation, but the entry in the books thereof at the College shows no tinctures.
