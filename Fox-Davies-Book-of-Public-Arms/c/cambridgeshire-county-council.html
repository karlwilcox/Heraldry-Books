CAMBRIDGESHIRE COUNTY COUNCIL. Azure, a bend wavy and a double tressure flory counterflory, both or. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a castle charged with an open helmet, both proper. Supporters— On either side a great bustard proper.

[Granted, College of Arms, 17th June 1914.]
