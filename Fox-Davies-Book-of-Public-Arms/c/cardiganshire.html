CARDIGANSHIRE. Has no amorial bearings. The seal of the County Council represents a view of the University College, Aberystwith, with the Motto

" Goreu arf, arf dysc." This, the editor is informed, is the Welsh for " The best weapon is the weapon of knowledge," another rendering, perhaps, of the ancient proverb, " Knowledge is power."
