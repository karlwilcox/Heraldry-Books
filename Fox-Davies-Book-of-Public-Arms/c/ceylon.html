CEYLON. Argent, on a mount vert between a grove of eight cocoanut trees and mountains in perspective an elephant affrontee all proper.

[Assigned by Royal Warrant, 17th December 1906.]

The Admiralty publish for use upon the Union Flag by the Governor of Ceylon, a device consisting of a disc azure, thereon on a mount vert, a temple, and in front thereof, an elephant proper, the whole within a circular band of red edged and ornamented with gold.
