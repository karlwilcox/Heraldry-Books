CHARLESTOWN (Aberlour). Has no arms. Those upon the seal are the arms of Grant of Elchies, "Gules, a boar's head between three antique crowns, or." Crest — An oak-tree, and above the Motto — " Craig a crochan." Under the arms, " Stand fast."
