CLARENCEUX KING OF ARMS. Argent, a cross gules, on a chief of the second, a lion passant guardant or, crowned of the last.

[These arms of office are either borne alone or impaled on the de.xter side of the personal arms of Clarenceux. The escutcheon is surmounted by his official crown.]
