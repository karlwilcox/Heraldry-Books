CLOGHER, See of Azure, a bishop in pontifical robes seated on his chair of state, and leaning towards the sinister, his left hand supporting a crozier, his right hand upraised in benediction, all or, the feet upon a cushion gules tasselled gold.

[This coat, which is recorded in Ulster's Office, remains in use, but through the disestablishment of the Irish Church it is really extinct and its present use is illegal.]
