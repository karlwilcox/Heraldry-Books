COACH AND COACH-HARNESS-MAKERS, The Worshipful Company of (London). (Incorporated 31st May 1677). Azure, a chevron between three coaches, or. Crest — On a wreath of their colours, a Phcjebus in his glory sitting in his chariot or, drawn through a cloud proper by four horses argent, housed, reined, and bridled, or. Supporters — Two horses argent, bridled and harnessed, sable, the harness studded or, garnished gules, and housed azure, with fringe and purfling or, adorned also with plumes of feathers or, azure, argent, and gules. Motto—" Surgit Post nubila Phcebus."

[Granted by Sir William Dugdale, Garter, and Sir Henry St George, Norroy, 17th July 1677.]
