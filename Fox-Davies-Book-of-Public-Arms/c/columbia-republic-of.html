COLUMBIA, REPUBLIC OF. Azure, on a fesse argent, a cap of liberty, gules, in chief a pomegranate or, seeded gules between two cornucopias proper, the base a landscape showing the Isthmus of Panama between two ships in full sail in the sea all proper.

[An earlier coat was decreed, 4th October 1821, as follows: "Two cornucopias filled with the fruits of the frigid, temperate, and torrid districts, surrounding the Columbian fasces, which shall be composed of a bundle of lances, and the battleaxe placed sideways, bows and arrows crossed in the centre, and tied below with a tri-coloured ribbon."]
