COVE AND KILCREGGAN. Has no arms. Those in use are argent, a representation of the Public Hall of the Burgh, on a chief azure an eagle displayed between two ancient Norse galleys. Crest — An eagle's head erased. Alotto —

"Aquila non captat muscas." [Quite bogus.]
