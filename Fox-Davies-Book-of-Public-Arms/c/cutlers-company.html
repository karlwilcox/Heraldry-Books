CUTLERS' COMPANY (Sheffield). (Incorporated by Act of Parliament, 24 Jas. I., c. 31.) Argent, on a fess indented vert, between three pairs of swords ill saltire proper, pommels and hilts sable, eight arrows interlaced saltirewise banded of the field between two garbs or. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, in front of an elephant's head couped or, two swords in saltire as in the arms. Motto — " Pour y parvenir a bon foi." [Granted College of Arms.]
