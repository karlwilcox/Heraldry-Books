DARWEN, OVER (Lancashire). Or, a fesse wavy with cottices also wavy azure, between three sprigs of the cotton-tree slipped and fructed proper. And for the Crest — On a wreath of the colours in front of a demi-miner habited proper, holding over his shoulder a pick or, a shuttle fessewise of the last, thread pendent proper. Motto — " Absque labore nihil."

Granted, College of Arms, August 7, 1S78.
