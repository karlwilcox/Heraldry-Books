DOWN AND CONNOR, See of. Azure, two keys indorsed in saltire or, suppressed by a lamb in fesse argent.

[This coat, which is recorded in Ulster's Office and also in the College of Arms, remains in use, but through the disestablishment of the Irish Church it is really extinct and its present use is illegal.]
