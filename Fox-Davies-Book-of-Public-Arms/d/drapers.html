DRAPERS, The Worshipful Company of (London). (Incorporated 15th July 1364.) Azure, three clouds proper, radiated in base or, each surmounted with a triple crown or, caps gules. Crest — On the wreath of the colours, a mount vert, thereon a ram couchant or, armed sable. Supporters — Two lions argent, pellette. Motto — " Unto God only be Honour and Glory."

[Arms granted by Sir William Bridges, Garter, 1439. Crest and Supporters granted by William Hervey, 1590; some alterations made by Sir William Segar, Garter, in 1614, and the whole approved and entered at the Visitation of the City of London by Henry St George, 1634.]
