DROMORE, See of Argent, sem^e of trefoils slipped vert, a cross patt^e gules, on a chief azure, the sun in splendour. Another coat, argent two keys in saltire the wards in chief gules, surmounted by an open book in fesse proper between two crosses pattee fitchce in pale sable.

[Both these coats are recorded in Ulster's Office, but through the disestablishment of the Irish Church they are really extinct, and their present use is illegal.]
