DUDLEY (Worcestershire). Has no armorial bearings. Debrett's " House of Commons " gives an illustration of the following, which appear upon the seal : —

" Gules on a fesse engrailed argent between in chief a representation of Dudley Castle, and in base a salamander in flames, a basket of coals (? a lump of iron ore or ? a fleur-de lis, or .'' a trilobite) between, on the dexter side an anchor, and on the sinister side a miner's safety lamp." Crest — A lion's head.
