EARL MARISCHAL OF SCOTLAND, Badge of Office. Two batons gules, semee of thistles or, each ensigncd with an imperial crown or, placed saltirewise behind his arms.

Badge of Office. Two batons of gold tipped with sable in saltire behind his arms.

[A deputy Earl Marshal places one baton as above in bend dexter behind his shield.]

[Both the foregoing are recorded in the College of Arms.]
