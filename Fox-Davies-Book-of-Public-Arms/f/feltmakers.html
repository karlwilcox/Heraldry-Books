FELTMAKERS, The WorshipfulCompany of (London). (Incorporated 2nd August 1604.) Argent, a dexter hand couped at the wrist gules, between two hat-bands nowed azure, in chief a hat sable banded of the third. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a naked arm embowed proper holding in the hand a hat sable, banded azure, lilotto — " Decus et tutamen." [Of no authority.]
