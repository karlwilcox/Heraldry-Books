FETHARD (Co. Wexford). (Incorporated 161 3.) Gules, Mars in complete armour sable, garnished or, stockings whitish, his shoes sable, his kilt azure, on his head a plume, on his sinister arm a round shield of St George, brandishing in his dexter hand a sword proper, the whole between two lions passant guardant or.

[Granted by Preston, Ulster King of Arms, April i, 1641.]
