FETTES COLLEGE (Edinburgh). Has no arms. Those in use are : Or, a chevron between in chief two mullets (of six points), and in base a cross crosslet fitch^e gules. Crest — A bee volant in pale. Motto — " Industria." Supporters — (Dexter) a lion rampant gules ; (sinister) a stag proper, collared and chained or. [These are the arms of the founder of the College, and are used intact, even to the inescutcheon of a Baronet upon the chevron.]
