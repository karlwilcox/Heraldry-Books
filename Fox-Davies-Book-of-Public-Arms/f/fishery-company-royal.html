FISHERY COMPANY, ROYAL, or ROYAL COMPANY OF FISHING.

Barry wavy of six argent and azure, an ancient galley with one mast, and pennon or. Crest — In a prince's coronet or, three tridents sable, points upwards gold. Supporters — (De.xter) a merman ; (sinister), a mermaid, both proper, and crined or, each holding in the exterior hand the Union banner. Motto — " Messis ab alto." [Granted by Walker, Garter, 13th December 1664.]
