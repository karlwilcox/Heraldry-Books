FLANDERS MERCHANTS, or BRABANT MERCHANTS. Azure and .silver undey, a chief quarterly, the first and fourth quarters gules, a leopard gold armed azure, the second and third quarters or, two roses gules.

[Recorded in the College of Arms. Refer to the New Adventurers or French Merchants Company, with which this may have had some connection.]
