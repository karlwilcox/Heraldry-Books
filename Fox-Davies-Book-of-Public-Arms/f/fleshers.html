FLESHERS (Butchers). Incorporated Trade (Edinburgh). Argent, two axes in saltire endorsed proper between three bulls' heads couped sable, on a chief azure, a boar's head couped between two garbs or.

[Not matriculated in Lyon Register. Refer sub Edinburgh. Berry suggests that the garbs should be block-brushes {i.e. bunches of holly) as in the arms of the Butchers' Company of London.]
