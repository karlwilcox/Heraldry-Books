FRANCE, King of. Anciently azure, seme de-lis or. Modern (changed by Charles IV.). Axure, three fleurs-de-lis or, [sometimes impaling gules, a double orle, saltire and cross, composed of chains from an annulet in the centre point or, for Navarre], over the escocheon a helmet or, edged and damasked, all open, mantled, or, azure, and gules, surmounted with a royal crown. The crest of France is a fleur-de-lis or.
