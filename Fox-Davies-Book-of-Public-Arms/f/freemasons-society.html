FREEMASONS' SOCIETY. Use the following arms, crest, and supporters, viz. — Sable on a chevron between three towers argent, a pair of compasses open chevronwise of the first. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a dove proper. Supporters — Two beavers proper.
