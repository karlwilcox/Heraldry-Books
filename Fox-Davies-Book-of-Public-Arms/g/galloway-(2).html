GALLOWAY, See of. Argent, St Ninian standing and full faced proper, clothed with a pontifical robe purple, on his head a mitre, and in his dexter hand a crozier or.

[These arms were matriculated in Lyon Register, c. 1675-9, and are still in use, but by the disestablishment of the Episcopal Church in Scotland they are really extinct and their present use is improper.]
