GALWAY (Co. Galway). Argent, on waves of the sea in base proper, a galley or with one mast and sails furled, the rigging charged with an escutcheon sable charged with a lion rampant or.

[Recorded in Ulster's Office by Christopher Ussher, c. 1678-98.]
