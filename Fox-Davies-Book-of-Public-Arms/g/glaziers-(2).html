GLAZIERS (Gateshead). Argent, two glazing irons in saltire between four closing nails sable on a chief gules, a lion passant guardant or. Crest — A lion's head couped between two wings expanded or. Supporters — Two naked boys proper, each holding a long torch inflamed or.

[Of no authority: taken from the Gateshead Charter, 1671.]
