GLENALMOND, Trinity College. Azure, a saltire argent, between the sun in his splendour in chief and a fleur-de-lis in base and two crescents in fesse or.

[Matriculated in Lyon Register, isth September 1898. The grant is printed in extenso in The Glenalmond Chronicle for January 1899.]
