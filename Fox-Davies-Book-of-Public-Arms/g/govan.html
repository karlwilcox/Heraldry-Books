GOVAN, Police Burgh of (Lanarkshire). The following Ensignes Armorial: Argent, the hull of a ship on the stocks proper, on a chief azure, two mullets pierced of the field. Above the shield is placed a suitable helmet with a mantling gules doubled argent, and on a wreath of the proper liveries is set for Crest, A garb surmounted by a salmon on its back proper, and in an escroll over the same this Motto, " Nihil sine labore," and on a compartment below the shield are placed for Supporters, On the dexter side, an engineer holding in his exterior hand a plan, and on the sinister a ship-carpenter resting his exterior hand on a mallet, both habited proper.

[Matriculated in Lyon Register, 7th June 1884.]
