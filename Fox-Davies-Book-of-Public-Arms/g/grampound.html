GRAMPOUND (Cornwall). Has no armorial bearings. The seal represents a bridge of two arches over a river, the dexter end in perspective showing the passage over, at the sinister end a tree issuing from the base against the bridge, on the centre an escutcheon of the arms of the family of Cornwall, namely, argent, a lion rampant gules within a bordure sable.
