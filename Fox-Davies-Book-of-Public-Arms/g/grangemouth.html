GRANGEMOUTH. Has no armorial bearings. The seal shows a shield per pale or, the dexter side a representation of "a primitive steamboat" ; sinister, the arms of Dundas, Lord Zetland. Ct'est — A steamboat. Motto — "Ingenium vincit omnia."
