GUILDHALL FRATERNITY (London). Azure, on a chief gules, a leopard's head cabossed or, langued gules, and in base a fleur-de-lis of the third, between two holy-water sprinklers in saltire also of the third, and argent. Crest — Six holy-water sprinklers in saltire or and argent, banded of the first. Mmitluig — Azure and gules furred with ermine.

[Granted by Holme, Clarenceux, July i6, 1482 (22 Edward IV.), and confirmed by Benolt, Clarenceux, 1530, 22 Henry VIII.]
