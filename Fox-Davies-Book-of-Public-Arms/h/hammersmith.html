HAMMERSMITH, Borough of (London). Party per pale azure and gules, on a chevron between two cross crosslets in chief and an escallop in base argent, three horse-shoes of the first. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, upon the battlements of a tower two hammers in saltire all proper. Motto — " Spectemur agendo."

[Granted 23rd December 1897.]
