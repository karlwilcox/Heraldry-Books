HAMPSTEAD, Borough of (London). Has no arms. Those in use are : Azure, on a cross argent, a mitre between four fleurs-de-lis gules, a chief indented or, fretty also gules. Crest — A buck's head couped argent, gorged with a wreath of holly fructed proper. Motto — " Non sibi sed toti." [Of no authority.]
