HERIOT'S TRUST, The Governors of George (Edinburgh). Have no arm.s. Those in use are "argent, on a fesse azure, three cinquefoils of the field, in base a mullet gules." Crest — A cornucopia proper. Motto — " I distribute cheerfully," or alternatively, " Impendo."

[George Heriot, jeweller to King James, born in Edinburgh, died in London, 1623. No arms for him or his family are matriculated in Lyon Register, but the shield only as above quoted is on record at the College of Arms in the Register of Funeral Certificates.]
