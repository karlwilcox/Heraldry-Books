HERTFORD COLLEGE (Oxford). No arms. ^^-^Z— Represented in a landscape a hart stooping down his head as going to drink at a ford, all within a ribbon, on which was the Motto — "Sicut cervus anheiat ad fontes aquarum."

According to the University Calendar the arms in use are : " Gules, a stag's head caboshed argent, attired and between the attires a cross pattee fitchee at the foot or," but there is no official authority for this.
