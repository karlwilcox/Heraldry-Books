HEYTESBURY (Wiltshire). Has no armorial bearings. The seal (according to Burke and Berry) shows the following arms ... a long cross mounted on three degrees, ensigned on the top with a fleur-de-lis, on each side of the cross an escutcheon, thereon a chief and two chevrons. Berry adds a note, " The colours are not known."
