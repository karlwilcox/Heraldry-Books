HIGHAM FERRERS (Northamptonshire). Has no armorial bearings. The Corporation seal, which is very ancient, represents in chief a dexter hand couped at the wrist, the little finger and the next doubled in, the others pointing to the dexter side, under the hand nine men's heads in profile couped at the neck, five in the upper row, the centre head looking to the dexter side, all the other eight looking to the centre of the seal.
