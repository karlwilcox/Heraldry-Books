INCORPORATED ACCOUNTANTS AND AUDITORS, Society of. Have no arms. The device in use is a female figure vested, crowned with a mural crown, holding in her dexter hand a scroll inscribed " Diligentia et vigilantia," and in her sinister hand a key, and standing on the upper part of a terrestrial globe issuing amongst clouds.
