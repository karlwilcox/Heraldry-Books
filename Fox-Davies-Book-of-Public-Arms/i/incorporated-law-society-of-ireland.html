INCORPORATED LAW SOCIETY OF IRELAND. Azure, a harp or,, stringed argent, on a chief ermine, a pale gules, charged with an Imperial crown proper. Mantling — Gules, doubled argent. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a Figure of Justice proper. Supporters — Two Irish wolf-hounds or. Motto—" Veritas vincet."

[Granted by Ulster King of Arms, 7th June 191 2.]
