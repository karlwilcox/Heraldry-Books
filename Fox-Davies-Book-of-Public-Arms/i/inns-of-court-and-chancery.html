INNS OF COURT AND CHANCERY. Refer to Barnards, Chester or Strand, Clement's, Clifford's, Cursitor's, Furnival's, Gray's, Kidderminster or Six Clerks' Office, Lincoln's, Lion's, New or Our Lady's, Serjeant's, Stafford's, Staple's, Inner Temple, Middle Temple, Thavies.

Of the foregoing only Lincoln's Inn, Gray's Inn, and the Inner and Middle Temples remain in existence.
