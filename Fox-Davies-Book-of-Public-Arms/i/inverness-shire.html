INVERNESS-SHIRE. Has no armorial bearings. The seal of the County Council displays upon a trefoil a stag's head and a bull's head, both erased, and a lymphad. Motto — " Air son math na siorrachd." Legend — " Seal of the County Council of Inverness-shire."
