INVERNESS, Borough of (Inverness-shire). Gules, our Lord upon the cross proper. Mantling — Gules, doubled or. Crest. — Upon a wreath of the proper liveries, a cornucopia proper and in an escroll over the same this Motto —

" Concordia et fidelitas." Sxtpporters — (Dexter) a dromedary, (sinister) an elephant, both proper.

[Matriculated in Lyon Register, gth February 1900.]
