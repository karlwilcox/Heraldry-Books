IRON AND STEEL INSTITUTE (London). Sable, a buck's head caboshed and in chief two hawks' bells argent, on a chief rayonne or, the astronomical symbol of Mars of the first. Crest — A miner's pick and gad in saltire sable. Motto — " Faber fabrum adjuvet."

[Granted, College of Arms, March 3, 1908.]
