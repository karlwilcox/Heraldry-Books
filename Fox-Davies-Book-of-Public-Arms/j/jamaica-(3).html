JAMAICA, Churchwardens of St James, in. Argent, a palmer's staff erect, depending from its rest by a leathern thong, a gourd both proper, on a bordure gules five pine apples or.

\Vide Local Act, 7 Vict., cap. 39, cited (p. 4) in Roby's " History of the Parish of St James in Jamaica," 1849.]
