JESUS' COLLEGE, Oxford. (Founded by Queen Elizabeth, 1571.) Azure, three stags trippant argent, being the arms of Hugh Price, Doctor of Laws, who contributed largely to the building. According to the University Calendar the arms in use are " Vert three stags trippant or," which are the arms of Greene or Robinson. [Of no authority.]
