JOHNSTONE. Has no armorial bearings. The seal shows a cross between i, a spinning-wheel ; 2, a pair of scales; 3, a beam-engine; 4, a bee-hive. Crest — A lion rampant. Motto — " Gang forward." [Bogus.]
