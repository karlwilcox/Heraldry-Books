KELLIE, Earldom of. Gules, the Royal Crown of Scotland, within a double treasure, flory and counterflory or.

[This is a coat of augmentation for the Earldom of Kellie, matriculated in Lyon Register and borne surmounted by an Earl's coronet in the centre of their arms by the Earls of Mar and Kellie.]
