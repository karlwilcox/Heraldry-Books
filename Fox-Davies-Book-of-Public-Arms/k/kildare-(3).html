KILDARE, See of. Argent, a saltire engrailed gules, on a chief azure an open Bible proper garnished and clasped or, thereon the words in gold, " The Law was given by Moses, but grace and truth came by Jesus Christ."

[These arms are registered in Ulster's Office and in the College of Arms, but by the disestablishment of the Irish Church, legally they are now extinct.]
