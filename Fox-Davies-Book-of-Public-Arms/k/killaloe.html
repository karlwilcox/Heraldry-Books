KILLALOE, See of Ancient Arms — Argent a cross azure between four trefoils slipped vert, on a chief of the second a key in pale or. Modern Arms — Argent a cross gules between twelve trefoils slipped vert, on a chief azure a key in pale or.

[These last-mentioned arms are recorded in Ulster's Office and the modern coat remains in use, but through the disestablishment of the Irish Church are really extinct, and the present use is illegal.]
