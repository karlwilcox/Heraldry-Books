KILMARNOCK (Ayrshire). Has not matriculated any armorial bearings. Those in use at the present time are as follows : — Azure, a fesse chequy gules and argent. Crest — Upon a wreath of the colours, a dexter hand erect and apaumee, couped at the wrist, the third and fourth fingers folded down proper. Supporters — On either side a squirrel proper. Mottoes over the crest, " Confido," and under the arms, " Virtute et industria."
