KING'S COLLEGE (Cambridge). (Founded in 1441. by Henry VI.) Sable, three roses argent barbed vert, seeded or, on a chief per pale azure and gules a fleurde-lis on the dexter or, and a lion passant guardant on the sinister of the last.

[These arms were granted by King Henry VI. by Letters Patent under the Great Seal, 1441. See " Excerpta Historica," p. 362. Recorded, College of Arms.]
