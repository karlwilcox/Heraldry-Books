KNARESBOROUGH (Yorkshire). Has no armorial bearings. The seal represents a castle in base, on an escroll four letters, namely ERQR, over the castle, on a wreath a dexter hand in armour, couped at the wrist, holding a branch of acorns, the date i6i i.
