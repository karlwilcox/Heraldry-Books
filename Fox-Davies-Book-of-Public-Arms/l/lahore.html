LAHORE, See of. Azure, on a fesse ermine, a passion cross in bend dexter surmounted by a crozier in bend sinister or, in chief rising from behind two snow mountains issuing from the fesse a sun in splendour and in base five barrulets wavy argent.

[College of Arms. Gts., Ix. 96.]
