LEITH (Edinburghshire). Argent, in a sea proper, an ancient galley with two masts, sails furled sable, flagged gules, seated therein the Virgin Mary with the Infant Saviour in her arms and a cloud resting over their heads, all also proper. In an escroll below the shield is placed this motto, " Persevere."

[Matriculated in Lyon Register the 27th day of February 1889.]
