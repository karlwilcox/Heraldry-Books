LEVANT, OR TURKEY MERCHANTS, COMPANY. (Incorporated by Queen Elizabeth, 1579.) Azure, on a sea in base proper, a ship with three masts in full sail or, between two rocks of the second, all the sails, pennants, and ensigns argent, each charged with a cross gules, a chief engrailed of the third, in base a seahorse proper. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a demi seahorse saliant. Supporters — Two seahorses. Motto — " Deo reip et amicis." [Recorded in the College of Arms.]
