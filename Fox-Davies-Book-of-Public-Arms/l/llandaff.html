LLANDAFF (Glamorganshire). Has no armorial bearings, but Burke, in his

" General Armory," quotes, " Sa., two crosiers in saltire or, on a chief azure three mitres of the second." These, of course, are the arms of the See, with the exception that the sinister crozier should be argent.
