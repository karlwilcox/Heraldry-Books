LLANFYLLIN (Montgomeryshire). Has no armorial bearings, and, failing their possession, the corporation seal exhibits, with the legend, " Borough of Llanfyllin," the Royal arms, crown, supporters, garter, and motto, the arms being Quarterly i and 4 France and England quarterly, 2 Scotland, 3 Ireland.
