LUBECK (Germany). Per fesse argent and gules, the shield displayed on the breast of a double-headed eagle displayed sable, beaked and legged gules.

In the great shield of Lubeck, the eagle as above described is placed upon a shield or. Matitling — Gules and argent. Cirst — Out of a coronet or, a demieagle (with one head) displayed sable, beaked gules. Supporters — Two lions proper.
