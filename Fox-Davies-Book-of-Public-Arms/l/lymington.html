LYMINGTON (Hampshire). Has no armorial bearings. The seal represents on the sea a very antique ship of one mast, the sail furled ; and on the sinister side of the mast an escutcheon of the arms of Courtney, namely, " Or, three torteaux, a label of three points azure," with the legend, "Sigillum burgi de Lymington."
