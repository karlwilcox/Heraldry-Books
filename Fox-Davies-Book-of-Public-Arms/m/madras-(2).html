MADRAS, See of. Argent, on a mount vert, in front of a banyan tree, a kid on the dexter couchant looking towards the sinister, and on the sinister a leopard couchant guardant all proper, a chief azure, thereon a dove rising, in the beak an olive branch also proper between two crosses pattee or. [Recorded Heralds' Coll. Gts., xli. 67?^
