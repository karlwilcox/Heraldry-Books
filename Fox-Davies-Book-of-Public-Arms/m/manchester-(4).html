MANCHESTER, The Overseers of the Township of Or, a bale of cotton goods proper, on a chief azure between two garbs of the first, a pale argent, thereon an escutcheon gules, charged with three bendlets enhanced also of the first. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a female figure representing the Union of Justice with Charity, in her right hand a pair of scales and on her left arm an infant, all proper. Motto — "Justitia et benignitate." [Granted, College of Arms, 1S58.]
