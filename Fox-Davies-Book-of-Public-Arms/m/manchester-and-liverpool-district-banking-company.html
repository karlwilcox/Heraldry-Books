MANCHESTER AND LIVERPOOL DISTRICT BANKING COMPANY. Argent, two bendlets gules, a bordure azure, charged with seven bezants, a chief sable, thereon a garb between two fusils or. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, upon waves of the sea proper, an ancient ship with three masts, sails furled, colours flying, all or, between two coral branches proper. Motto— '' "Dczv^s prudentiae merces."

[Granted, College of Arms, 1S71.]
