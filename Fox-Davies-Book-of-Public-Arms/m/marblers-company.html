MARBLERS' COMPANY (London). Gules, a chevron argent, between two chipping axes in chief of the last and a mallet in base or. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, an arm embowed vested azure, cuffed argent, holding in the hand proper an engraving chisel of the last. Motto — " Grind well."

[Of no authority.]

[This Company was amalgamated with that of the Masons.]
