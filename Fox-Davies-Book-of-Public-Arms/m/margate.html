MARGATE (Kent). Per pale gules and azure, a chevron argent, between in chief a demi-lion passant guardant conjoined to the demi-hulk of a ship or, and in base a horse rampant of the third. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a seahorse supporting the mast of a ship, with yard and rigging all proper. Motto —

" Porta maris portus salutis."

Granted by Sir Charles George Young, Knt, Garter Principal King of Arms, J. Pulman, Clarenceux King of Arms, Robert Laurie, Norroy King of Arms, I2th January 1S58.
