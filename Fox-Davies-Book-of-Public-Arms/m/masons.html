MASONS, The Worshipful Company of, London. (Incorporated 17th December 1677). Sable, on a chevron engrailed between three antique castles argent, a pair of compasses expanded chevronwise of the first. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a castle argent. Motto — " God is our Guide." (Another, " In the Lord is all our trust."

[Recorded in the College of Arms.]
