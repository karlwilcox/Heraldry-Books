MIDLETON (Co. Cork). Has no armorial bearings registered in Ulster's Office. Lewis's " Topographical Dictionary," however, gives " Argent on a chief vert, two spear-heads of the first, the points embrued gules." These are, of course, the arms of Brodrick, Lords Midleton.
