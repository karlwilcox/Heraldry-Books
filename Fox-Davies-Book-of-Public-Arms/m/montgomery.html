MONTGOMERY (Montgomeryshire). Has no armorial bearings. The seal represents an escutcheon, and thereon two keys in saltire and endorsed ; and these are usually supposed to be the arms of the Borough. The legend is, " Sig. Balivorum et Burgensium Mountgomery."

Berry and Burke add a note, " By some of the Arms of the Town are represented to be az. a lion ramp, or, within a bordure of the last."
