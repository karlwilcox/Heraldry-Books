MORAY, ROSS AND CAITHNESS, Bishop of. According to Crockford the arms in use are divided per fesse and the chief per pale, in the dexter chief the arms of the See of Moray (to which refer), in the sinister chief the arms of the See of Ross (to which refer), and in base the arms of Caithness. This device is, of course, quite unauthorised.
