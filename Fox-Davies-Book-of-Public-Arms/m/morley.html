MORLEY (Yorkshire). Argent, on a fesse gules, between a sprig of the cottontree slipped, fructed, and leaved proper between two pellets in chief, and a pickaxe surmounted by a spade in saltire in base sable, a shuttle fessewise or, thread pendant of the first ; and for the Crest — Upon a wreath of the colours, in front of a ram's head couped argent, a shuttle fessewise proper, thread pendant, also argent. Motto — " Industria omnia vincit."

[Granted, College of Arms, gth August 1887.]
