MULLINGAR (Co. Westraeath). Has no armorial bearings registered in Ulster's Qfifice. The coat attributed to the town in the sheet of Irish arms published by Marcus Ward & Co., Ltd., the design of which is taken, I believe, from the seal, is beyond my powers to blazon.
