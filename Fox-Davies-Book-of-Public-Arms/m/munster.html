MUNSTER, Bishopric of. Quarterly of six, three and three: first and sixth, per fesse argent and gules on the fesse line, three birds issuant to the sinister sable (for Stromberg) ; second and fifth, azure a fesse or (for Munster) ; third and fourth (Borkelo) ; over all an escocheon argent.
