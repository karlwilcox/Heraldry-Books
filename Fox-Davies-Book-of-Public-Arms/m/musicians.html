MUSICIANS, The Worshipful Company of, London. (Incorporated 9th Edward IV.) Azure, a swan with wings expanded argent, within a double tressure flory counterflory or, on a chief gules, a pale between two lions passant guardant or, thereon a rose of the fourth, seeded of the third, barbed vert. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a lyre or. Motto — " Harmonj'." [Granted by William Camden, Clarenceux, October 1604.]
