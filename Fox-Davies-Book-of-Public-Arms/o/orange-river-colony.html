ORANGE RIVER COLONY (South Africa). " Argent, on a mount, a springbuck, and on a chief azure, the Imperial Crown all proper."

[Assigned by Royal Warrant. As the Orange River Free State the badge or device of an orange tree appeared on its postage stamps, and this survives in the arms recently assigned to the Union of South Africa. Refer also to Union of South Africa. The device published by the Admiralty for use by the Governor on the Union flag is a landscape disc thereon (? a gemsbok).]
