ORDNANCE, Master of King Charles II. granted a Warrant, December 1683, to George, Lord Dartmouth, to enable him as Master of the Ordnance to bear on each side of his arms a field-piece mounted, to show the honour of his office, which said warrant was made to extend to his successors in that department.
