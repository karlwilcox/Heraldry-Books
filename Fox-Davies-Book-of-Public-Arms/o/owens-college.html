OWENS COLLEGE (Manchester). Now extended into the Victoria University of Manchester. Argent, a serpent nowed vert, on a chief nebulee azure, a sun issuant or. Crest — Between two branches of laurel a palm tree proper, suspended in front thereof by a riband azure, a shield argent, thereon a lion rampant gules and a chief of the last charged with three bendlets or. Motto — "Arduus ad solem."

[Granted 14th October 1871.]
