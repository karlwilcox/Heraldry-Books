PAINTERS, or PAINTER-STAINERS, The Worshipful Company of, London. (Incorporated 1467.) Quarterly i and 4 azure, three escutcheons, two and one argent, 2 and 3 azure, a chevron between three phoenix heads erased or. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a phoenix or, in flames proper. Supporters — Two leopards argent, spotted with various colours, ducally crowned, collared and chained or. Motto—'' Amor et obedientia."

[Granted by Thomas Holme, Clarenceux, i486. Confirmed by Benolt, Clarenceux, nth October 1531.]
