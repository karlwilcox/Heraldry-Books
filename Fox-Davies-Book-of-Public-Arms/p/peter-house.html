PETER HOUSE, or ST PETER'S COLLEGE (Cambridge). (Founded in 1256 by Hugh de Balesham, or Balsham, Bishop of Ely.) Or, three palets gules, a bordure of the last charged with eight ducal coronets of the first. [Recorded in the College of Arms.]
