PEWTERERS, The Worshipful Company of, London. (Incorporated 20th January 1473.) Azure, on a chevron or, between three cross-bars of pewter (antique limbecks) argent, as many roses gules seeded of the second and barbed vert. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a mount vert, thereon two arms embowed vested argent, cuffed gules, holding in both hands a pewter dish of the third. Supporters — Two sea-horses or, their tails proper. Motto — " In God is all my Trust."

[Granted 20th May 1479.]
