PRINTERS' GUILD (Vienna). Or, the double-headed eagle of the RomanGerman Empire, the heads each within a nimbus and armed gules, holding in the dexter claw a leaf-holder and in the sinister a composing-stick. Mantling — Gules and argent. Crest — Out of a coronet, a denii-griffin argent, armed gules, holding in its claws two printing balls, one above the other, the heads conjoined.
