PRUSSIA, EAST (Province of). Argent, an eagle displayed sable, crowned, beaked, legged, and with sachsen or, holding in the dexter claw a sceptre and in the sinister an orb proper, and charged on the breast with a cypher or. Mantling— ^-dkA^ and argent. Crest — On a coronet or, an eagle displayed as in the arms. Stipporters—{T)exter) a wild man wreathed about the head and middle with oakleaves ; (sinister) a man in complete armour, each holding in his exterior hand a banner of the arms.
