QUEBEC, See of Per fesse wavy azure and gules, in chief a book open proper, clasped and ornamented gold, upon the book a crosier in bend or, in base a lion passant guardant of the fourth, holding in the dexter paw a key erect argent, on a canton of the last a cross of the second between four crosses patee fitchde sable.

[College of Arms. Gts., xviii. 252.]
