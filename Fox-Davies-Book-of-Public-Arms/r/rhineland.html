RHINELAND, Province of (Prussia). Argent, the Prussian eagle, on its breast a crowned inescutcheon vert, thereon a bend wavy of the field. Crest — Out of a crown or two wings vert, each charged with a bend wavy argent. Supporters — (Dexter) a savage supporting a banner of Prussia ; (sinister) a man in complete armour, supporting a banner of the Province as above.
