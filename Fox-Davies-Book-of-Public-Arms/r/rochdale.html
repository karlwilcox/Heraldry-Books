ROCHDALE (Lancashire). Argent, a vtrool-pack encircled by two branches of the cotton-tree flowered and conjoined proper, a bordure sable, charged with eight martlets of the field. Crest — Upon a wreath of the colours, a mill-rind sable, and above a fleece argent banded or. Motto—" Crede Signo."

Granted by Sir Charles George Young, Knt, Garter Principal King of Arms; J. Pulman, Clarenceux King of Arms; Robert Laurie, Norroy King of Arm.s, 20th February 1857.
