ROXBURGH, County of. The County of Roxburgh bears azure, an unicorn saliant argent, horned, maned, and unguled or, the tail tufted of the last on a chief of the second, a hunting-horn sable stringed and viroled gules, between two esquires' helmets of the field. Crest — A dexter arm from the shoulder vambraced and brandishing a scymitar aloft proper, the last hilted and pommelled or. Motto — (below the shield) " Ne cede malis sed contra audentior ito."

[Matriculated in Lyon Office, 9th July 1798.]
