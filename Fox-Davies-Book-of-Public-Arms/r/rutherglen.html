RUTHERGLEN (Lanarkshire). Argent, in a sea proper an ancient galley sable, flagged gules, therein two men proper, one rowing, the other furling the sail. Above the shield is placed a suitable helmet, with a mantling gules doubled argent, and on a wreath of the proper liveries is set for Crest— A demi-figure of the Virgin Mary with the Infant Saviour in her arms proper, and on a compartment below the shield, on which is an escroll containing this Motto — " Ex fumo fama," are placed for Supporters — Two angels proper winged or.

[Arms matriculated in Lyon Office, and the supporters granted 4th April 1889. Wm. Mitchell, Esquire, Provost]
