SCOTLAND, Company of, trading to Africa and the Indies. Azure, a saltire argent, between a ship under sail flagged of Scotland in chief proper, a Peruvian sheep in base, a camel on the dexter and an elephant on the sinister [proper], the first two of these loaded and' the last bearing a turret of the second. Crest — A rising sun. Sitpporters — De.xter an Indian, .sinister a Negro " au naturel," each bearing on his shoulder a cornucopia with this motto in an escroU above,

" Qua panditur orbis," and in the table of the compartment this symbol, " Vis unita fortior."

[Matriculated in Lyon Register, loth July 1696.]
