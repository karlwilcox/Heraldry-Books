SECRETARIES, CHARTERED INSTITUTE OF (London). Ermine, on a pale engrailed azure, between two keys in pale wards downward or, a quill pen palewise argent. Crest — On a key fessewise, wards downward and to the sinister or, a Secretary bird close proper. Motto — " Semper vigilans." [Granted, College of Arms, May 2, 1903.]
