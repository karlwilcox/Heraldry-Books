SERAMPORE COLLEGE (Bengal). Argent, a cross gules, on a chief azure, an open book or, the pages argent, between two crosses pattee gules, pierced of the first, fimbriated of the fourth. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, upon clouds, a pelican in her piety all proper. Motto — " Gloriam sapientes possidebunt." [Granted, College of Arms, 4th April 191 3.]
