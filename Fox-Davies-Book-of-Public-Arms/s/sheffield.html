SHEFFIELD, See of. Azure, a crosier in pale ensigned by a fleur-de-lis between in fesse a key surmounted by a sword in saltire to the dexter and to the sinister eight arrows interlaced and banded saltirewise, all or. [Granted, College of Arms, 21st April 191 4.]
