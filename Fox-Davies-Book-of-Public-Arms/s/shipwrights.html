SHIPWRIGHTS, The Worshipful Company of, London. (Incorporated 1905.) Azure, in the sea the hulk of a ship or, on a chief argent, a cross gules, charged with a lion passant guardant or. Crest — On a wreath or and azure, on an ark sable, resting on a mount vert, a dove proper, bearing an olive branch. Motto—'' Within the ark safe for ever."

[Arms granted, College of Arms, 1605.]

[Berry blazons this coat "azure, an antique hulk, the stern terminating with the head of a dragon, in the hulk the ark with three doors in the side, from the ark against the side a step ladder all or, on a chief argent," etc., and he makes the ark in the crest gold.]
