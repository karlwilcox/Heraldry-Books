ST. DAVID'S, College of. Sable, between four cinquefoils in cross or, a figure representing St David standing in his archiepiscopal robes in a niche under a canopy, holding in his dexter hand a crosier and in his sinister a book, all gold. Motto^-" Gair duw gorew dysg."

[College of Arms. Gts. xxxviii. 70.]
