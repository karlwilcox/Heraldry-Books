STAPLE MERCHANTS OF LONDON. (Incorporated by Edward III., whose reign they held their staple for Wool at Calais, from whence it was removed to England in the year 13S9.) Barr}' nebuly of si.x argent and azure, on a chief gules, a lion passant guardant or. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a ram argent, armed and unguled or. Supporters — Two rams argent, armed and unguled or. Motto — " God be our friend." [Recorded in the College of Arms.]
