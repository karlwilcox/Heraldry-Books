STONEHAVEN (Co. Kincardine). Has no arms. The seal Aows what is intended for the arms, crest, supporters, and motto of the Earls Marischal, but what actually is on the seal is, Azure, a chief paly of six or and argent. Crest — Issuant from a coronet a stag's head. Supporters — Two stags. Motto — "Veritas vincit."
