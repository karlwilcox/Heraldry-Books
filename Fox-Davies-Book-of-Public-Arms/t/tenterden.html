TENTERDEN (Kent). Gules, in base waves of the sea proper, and thereon a ship of three masts or, the sail on the fore-mast furled, the main-sail set, and bearing the arms of Sandwich, namely, party per pale gules and azure, three demi-lions passant guardant or, conjoined to as many hulks of ships argent, the mizzen charged with the arms, argent, on a bend sable between four lions' heads erased gules, three mullets or.

[Recorded in the College of Arms.]
