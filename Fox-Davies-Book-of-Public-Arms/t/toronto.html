TORONTO (West Canada), See of. Azure, a crozier in bend dexter surmounted by a key in bend sinister or, between an imperial crown in chief, two open books in fesse proper, and a dove rising in base argent, holding in the beak an olive branch vert.

[Recorded in College of Arms. Gts., xliv. 94.]
