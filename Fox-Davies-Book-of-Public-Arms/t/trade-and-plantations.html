TRADE AND PLANTATIONS, Commissioners of. Sea/— On a sea, two threemasted vessels completely rigged and under full sail, in base ; on the sinister side an island, and thereon the emblematical figure of Britannia, holding upright in her right hand an olive branch, her left hand supporting a spear erect, surmounted with a cap of liberty, and her arm resting on a shield, charged with the union cross, and near it several bales of goods lying on the ground ; over all, the legend — " Trade and Plantations."
