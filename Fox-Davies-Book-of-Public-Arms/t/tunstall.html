TUNSTALL (Staffordshire, now incorporated with Stoke-on-Trent). Has no armorial bearings, but the following were used ... on a chevron between in the sinister chief a soup-tureen and a vase in base, a SlafTord knot between two scythes on a canton . . . two furnaces. [Of no authority.]
