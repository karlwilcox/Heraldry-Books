TURKS' AND CAICOS ISLANDS. No warrant has been issued assigning arms to these islands. Though they have a separate administration they are in some ways annexed to Jamaica. The device issued by the Admiralty is a threemasted ship in full sail on the sea, in the foreground the seashore, etc.
