TWICKENHAM, Urban District Council of. Argent, a pall vert, between in chief an antique lamp fired proper, on the dexter side two swords in saltire also proper, pomelled and hilted or, and on the sinister side three roses two and one gules. Crest — Upon waves of water, a swan holding in the beak an eel all proper. Motto — " Looking backward, looking forward." [Granted, College of Arms, 30th October 1913.]
