UNIVERSITY OF LONDON. Argent, on a cross gules, the union rose irradiated and ensigned with the imperial crown proper, a chief azure, thereon an open book also proper, the clasps gold.

[Granted loth April 1838, by Ralph Bigland, Garter, William Woods, Clarenceux, and Edmund Lodge, Norroy.]
