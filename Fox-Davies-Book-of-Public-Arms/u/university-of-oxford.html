UNIVERSITY OF OXFORD. Azure, on a book open proper, leathered gules garnished or, on the dexter side thereof seven seals of the last, between three open crowns of the same the words " In p'ncipia erat vcrbu. et verbu. erat apud deu." These words have been frequently changed for " Dominus illuminatio mea" or for " Sapientia felicitas."

[Recorded in the College of Arms, Vn. of Oxford, 1574.]
