UNIVERSITY OF ST ANDREWS. Parted per saltire argent and azure, in chief a book expanded proper, leaved gules, and in base a lion rampant of the last armed and langued of the second, on a chief of the fourth a crescent reversed of the first between two mascles or.

[Matriculated, Lyon Register, November 24, 1905.]
