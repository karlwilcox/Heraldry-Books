UNIVERSITY OF WALES. Argent, on a fesse murrey three medieval lamps or, all within a bordure of the second charged with eight mullets of the third. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a dragon statant gules resting the dexter claw on an open book proper, inscribed with the words, " Gorev awen gwirioned." Motto — " Scientia ingenium artes."

[Granted, College of Arms, May 2, 1910.]
