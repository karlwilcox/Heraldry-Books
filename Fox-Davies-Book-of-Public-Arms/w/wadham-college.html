WADHAM COLLEGE (Oxford). (Founded in 161 3 by Nicholas Wadham, Esq., of Merefield, Co. Somerset, and Dorothy, his wife, sister of John, Lord Petre.) Gules, a chevron between three roses argent barbed vert, for Wadham ; impaling gules, a bend or, between two escallops argent, for Petre. [Of no authority.]
