WALLASEY, Borough of (Cheshire). Or, on waves of the sea a three-masted ship in full sail proper, on a chief azure to the dexter three garbs, two and one of the first, and to the sinister a bugle-horn proper, stringed and garnished gold Crest — On a wreath of colours, a dolphin head downwards proper entwining a trident erect or. Motto — " Audemus dum cavemus." [Granted, College of Arms, Septembers, 1910.]
