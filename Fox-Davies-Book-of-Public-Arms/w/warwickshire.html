WARWICKSHIRE. Has no armorial bearings. The following are, however, given on a sheet published under the title "Arms of the Counties of England and Wales," namely, Gules, a tower between on the dexter side a sun in splendour or, and on the sinister a crescent argent. This is, of course, a travesty on the seal of Warwick.
