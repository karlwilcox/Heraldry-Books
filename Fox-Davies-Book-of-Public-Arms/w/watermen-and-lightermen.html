WATERMEN AND LIGHTERMEN, The Worshipful Company of, London. (Incorporated 14th June 1827.) Barry wavy of six argent and azure, a boat or, on a chief of the second two oars in saltire of the third between as many cushions of the first tasselled or. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, a dexter arm embowed proper, vested argent, holding in the hand an oar erect or. Motto — (Over crest) " At command of our Superiors." Supporters — Two dolphins azure, finned or.

[Granted 18th September 1555. Confirmed at the visitation of London,
