WEDNESBURY (Staffordshire). Sable, on a fesse between two lions passant argent, crowned or, the emblem of Mars, between two lozenges of the field. And for the Crest — On a wreath of the colours, in front of the rising sun or, a tower with flames of fire proper, and charged with the emblem of Mars as in the arms, with the Motto — " Arte marte vigore."

[Recorded in the College of Arms. Gts., 74, 285.]
