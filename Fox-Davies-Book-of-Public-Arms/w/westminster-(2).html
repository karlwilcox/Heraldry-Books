WESTMINSTER, Marquessate of. The arms of the City of Westminster [to which refer] are borne by Royal Licence, quarterly (in the first and fourth quarters) with the arms of Grosvenor as a coat of augmentation by the Marquesses (now Dukes) of Westminster.
