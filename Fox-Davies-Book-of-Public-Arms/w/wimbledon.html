WIMBLEDON, Borough of (Surrey). Argent, a double-headed eagle displayed sable, beaked and legged gules, langued azure, charged on the dexter wing with a rose and on the sinister wing with a fret, both or, a bordure compony of the last and azure. Crest — Out of a mural crown a garb or supported on either side by a Cornish chough proper, beaked and legged gules. Motto— "Z'm& labe

decus."

[Granted, College of Arms, October 15, 1906.]
