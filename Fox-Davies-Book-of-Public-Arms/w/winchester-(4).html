WINCHESTER, Dean of. A minster or church argent, masoned sable, in the gate of the church the Holy Image of the Blessed Trinity gold and silver, crowned imperial on a canton per pale or and argent, a rose counterchanged. Recorded in the College of Arms, but it seems more probable that this is a seal device and not a coat-of-arms. Woodward gives the arms of the see and in chief the letter D, or, but there is no authority for this.
