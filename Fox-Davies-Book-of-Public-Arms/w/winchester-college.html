WINCHESTER COLLEGE. Sable, three lilies argent. [Recorded in the College of Arms.] These arms, however, do not appear to be used. Either the arms of Wykeham (argent, two chevronels sable between three roses gules, seeded or, barbed vert) or the arms of the See of Winchester impaling the arms of Wykeham appear to be made use of There is no authority for either of the last two versions. The Motto of the the School is " Manners Makyth Man."]
