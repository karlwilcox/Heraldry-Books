WOLVERHAMPTON, Borough of (Staffordshire). Gules, a cross formee or, between a pillar in the first quarter, a woolpack in the second, an open book in the third all argent, and in the fourth a padlock of the second. Crest — On a wreath of the colours, in front of a beacon sable, fired proper, two keys in saltire, wards upwards or. Motto — "Out of darkness cometh light." [Granted 31st December 1898.]
