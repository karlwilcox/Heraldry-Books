Elm-tree

Elm-tree: this tree is found, and the leaves also, the latter rather frequently.

Per fesse azure and argent, a fesse counter embattled or; in chief a mullet of six points of the second; in base on a mount vert an elm tree proper--OLMIUS, London.

Ermine, on two bars sable six elm leaves or, three and three--ELMES, Lincolnshire.

Sable, three bars engrailed between ten elm leaves erect or--ELMSALL, Yorkshire.

The leaves are also borne by the families of WALLER, Devon; FOLLIFORD, Suffolk; MURVESSE, Suffolk; ELLAMES, and several branches of the family of ELMES.
