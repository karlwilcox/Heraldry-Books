Regalia

Regalia: a name given to the crown, orb, sceptre, &c. The blazon of Earl MANDEVILLE'S arms, however, is perhaps erroneous.

Per pale or and gules; the regalia sable--MANDEVILLE, Earl of Essex, [according to Burke].
